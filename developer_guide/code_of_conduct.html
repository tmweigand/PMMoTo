Code of ConductÔÉÅ

PMMoTo is committed to providing a welcoming and respectful community for all contributors, users, and collaborators.

All participants are expected to:

  • Treat everyone with respect, regardless of background, experience, or identity.

  • Communicate constructively and politely.

  • Give and receive feedback gracefully.

  • Report any behavior that violates this code to the project maintainers.

Unacceptable behavior includes, but is not limited to:

  • Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form.

  • Offensive comments or language.

  • Public or private harassment of any community member.

  • Disruption of project activities or communities.

Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the project maintainers via email at: timothy.weigand@unc.edu

All reports will be handled with confidentiality and seriousness.

Enforcement

Project maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including removal from the community or revocation of contributor privileges, to ensure a safe and welcoming environment.

Acknowledgment

By participating in the PMMoTo community, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct.
